## Pattern Behind The Winning Pages

The pages that attract searches are not just famous designer names. They usually combine these traits:

1. High original price
   - The best pages sit in the expensive designer/niche range, usually Rs. 15,000+ original price.
   - This creates obvious "alternative", "clone", "inspired by", and "similar to" search intent.

2. Strong male fragrance identity
   - Current winners are masculine, bold, and recognizable.
   - Examples: tobacco, oud, leather, incense, amber, spicy vanilla, smoky resin.

3. Beast-mode or strong performance reputation
   - `10-12 hours`, `Strong`, `Date Night`, `Events`, `Evening`, `Fall/Winter/Monsoon` repeat across the winners.
   - Users and AI assistants like recommending these because performance is easy to explain.

4. Niche prestige with less SEO competition than mass designers
   - Mancera and Amouage are searched by fragrance-aware users, but fewer Indian stores have clean answer pages for them.
   - This gives HUME a better chance than fighting only for Dior Sauvage or Bleu de Chanel.

5. Clear India value angle
   - "Same scent direction at Rs. 799" or "save Rs. 14,000+" is very strong for AI answer engines.
   - The page template already says formulated for Indian heat/humidity, which helps local relevance.

6. The best targets map to an existing HUME product
   - Red Tobacco maps to `spicebomb`.
   - Interlude Man maps to `ombre-nomade`.
   - This lets the page answer and sell in one step.

## Add More Pages Like This

Prioritize new inspired-by pages when a fragrance has:

- Original price above Rs. 15,000.
- Strong Indian search intent around `clone`, `alternative`, `inspired`, `similar`, or `dupe`.
- Bold male/niche profile: oud, tobacco, leather, amber, vanilla, incense, smoky, spicy.
- A clear HUME product match.
- A simple page slug that matches how people search.

## Page Content Rule

For new pages, keep the answer direct:

- H1: `HUME Product: Inspired by Original Name`
- Quick answer near top.
- Price comparison.
- Note pyramid.
- "What makes it iconic".
- "How HUME captures it".
- "Formulated for India".
- FAQ with exact original fragrance name.

The page should satisfy both human shoppers and AI crawlers in the first viewport.
